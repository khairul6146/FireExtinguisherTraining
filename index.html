3. Fire Blanket

Selimut Api - Essential for Class K
🍳

Kitchen Fires (Class K)

Best for cooking oil fires in canteens. Cover the pot completely to starve fire of oxygen.

Gunakan untuk api minyak masak.

🏃

Personal Safety

Wrap around a person whose clothes are on fire. Stop, Drop, and Roll.

Balut mangsa yang terbakar pakaian.
